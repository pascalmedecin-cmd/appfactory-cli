- **[WATCH] Brand type `FeedbackEntry.context` vs `Database['Row'].context: Json`** (audit contracts S186 Low #4) : `types.ts:24` exige `FeedbackContext` non-nullable, mais `Database['feedback_entries']['Row'].context: Json`.
